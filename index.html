Aquila Scottish Golf Tour
Feedback Questionnaire

Dear Gentlemen,

Thank you for participating in our recent golf trip. As our first group, your involvement has been invaluable in helping us shape the Aquila Foundation's friend-raising and fundraising.

The Aquila Foundation is dedicated to providing churches in Scotland, England, and Wales with permanent homes. This mission is both urgent and immense, requiring us to raise over $40 million in the next five years. The golf trip was designed to let you see first-hand the work Aquila is involved in while also fostering meaningful connections—both among ourselves and with those Christian brothers and sisters who share a passion for God's work in the UK.

Your feedback has already provided crucial insights for refining our case statement, and we hope you will continue to partner with us as "friend-raisers" and even fundraisers. By connecting us with others who have a heart for this mission, you play a pivotal role in helping us reach those who can provide the resources necessary to fulfill God's calling.

Below, you'll find a short survey about your experience during the golf trip. Your feedback will help us improve these trips as we continue to engage potential supporters.

Thank you for your time, your insights, and your support of the Aquila Foundation's mission.

With gratitude,

Steve Wright
On behalf of the Aquila Foundation Board
